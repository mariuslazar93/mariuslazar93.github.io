about me

Hi. Marius here.

:romania: Romanian guy.
:heart: Loves his wife and JavaScript (order intended).
:uk: Lives in Birmingham, UK.

I like to code and I usually do it in JavaScript. I have a few years of experience building features for clients and side projects for myself and my friends.

My work stack is Angular(1.6 and 7+) on the frontend and Serverless Framework on top of the AWS infrastructure on the backend. Pretty much a modern stack, with bits and pieces to fix and maintain in a legacy app.

Generally I'm obsessed with learning, so I'm usually trying out new technologies or features of a language, I read most of the newsletters related to JavaScript and Nodejs and I try to listen to a podcast every now and then.

In my free time, I like to travel and meet new people. Me and my wife kinda have a hipster vibe, so we like unconventional methods of traveling like couchsurfing, workaway or hitchhiking. I enjoy spending time in nature, being it hiking or just hanging out with friends on a walk. I love coffee and board games and sometimes, cooking. Recently, I got more interested into wine, so cheers!
